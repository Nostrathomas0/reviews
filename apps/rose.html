General info

One of the old language learning companies, Rosetta Stone has been developing language, literacy and brain-fitness software since 1992.

24 Languages available; Spanish, English, Arabic, Chinese, Dutch, French, Italian, German, Japanese, Portuguese, Polish, Filipino, Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Russian, Turkish, Irish, Korean, Persian, Swedish & Vietnamese.

110$ annually, but the demo will show you that it's full of topic based units, each with 3 core lessons plus excertizes on pronunciation, vocabulary and grammar. Monthly 10$ (full 11$), Yearly 45$ -80$
